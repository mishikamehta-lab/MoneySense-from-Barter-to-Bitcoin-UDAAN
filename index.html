üí° WHAT IS FINANCE?

Finance is the process of managing money, credit, and investments to solve problems related to resource allocation, risk, and future value. Financial experts combine math, data, and strategy to build smart solutions for individuals, businesses, and governments.

Examples: Saving accounts, loan interest, budgeting apps, stock market investing, and government taxes.

  • Metaverse Asset Evaluator (Assesses value of digital land and virtual goods)
  • Cyber Risk Underwriter (Insures businesses against hacking losses)

ü§ñ FINANCE & AI

AI can process billions of data points and speed up repetitive work like fraud detection and algorithmic trading, but it cannot handle ethical dilemmas, build complex client trust, or understand human emotions that drive market panic. Financial professionals will use AI as a powerful tool‚Äîto analyze data, simulate risks, and speed up reporting. Human judgment, ethics, and strategic planning remain the most important parts of finance.
